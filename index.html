"When you approach this instrument for the first time, what comes out of you is simply what you feel." - Dennis Chambers.

Ireland's Premier Drum School

CPS is the school of choice in Ireland for those wishing to begin, advance, and hone the craft of drumming. Students are taught in groups of 6, or 1-1 settings, and will learn all aspects of drumming and specialise in one of 8 genres:

  • Blues
  • Bossanova
  • Folk
  • Funk
  • Jazz
  • Metal
  • Reggae
  • Rock

The finest instructors

Our instructors demonstrate varied styles and multi-cultural influences. CPS is the one-stop-shop for all your drumming knowledge.

Weekly drum clinics

These are shows designed to exhibit the various styles from around the world, and allow students to meet with experienced professionals. We've even hosted Danny Carey and Stuart Copeland!

State of the art facilities

With state of the art facilities, students can shoose to either practice alone in one of our pods, or learn/collaborate with fellow students.
