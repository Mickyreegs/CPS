Ireland's Premier Drum School

Since its creation in 2006, CPS has become the school of choice in Ireland for those wishing to begin, advance, and hone the craft of drumming. With an award-winning curriculum that is taught in groups of 6 or 1-1 settings, students will learn all aspects of drumming and specialise in one of 8 genres:

  • Blues
  • Bossanova
  • Folk
  • Funk
  • Jazz
  • Metal
  • Reggae
  • Rock

Learn from the finest instructors

Our instructors are professionals and ex-students who have been drumming for at least 15 years each. With varied styles and multi-cultural influences, CPS is the one-stop-shop for all your drumming knowledge.

Weekly drum clinics hosted by professionals

Students can partake or watch the weekly drum clinics hosted by industry professionals. These are shows designed to exhibit the various styles from around the world, and allow students meet with experienced professionals and learn from them. We've even hosted Danny Carey and Stuart Copeland!

Collaborate with fellow students

With state of the art facilities, students can shoose to either practice alone in one of our pods, or collaborate/battle with fellow students to test their abilities or learn from peers.
